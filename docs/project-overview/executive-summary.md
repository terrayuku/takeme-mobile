# Executive Summary

## Project Name
TakeMe Mobile

## Vision
To revolutionize public transportation navigation in South Africa by creating a crowdsourced platform that helps commuters easily find taxi routes through shared destination sign photos.

## Problem Statement
In South Africa, the minibus taxi industry serves millions of commuters daily, but there's no centralized system to help passengers identify which taxis go to their desired destinations. Commuters rely on memorizing taxi signs or asking drivers, creating confusion and inefficiency.

## Solution
TakeMe Mobile is a crowdsourced mobile application that allows users to:
- Search for taxi routes by entering origin and destination
- View photos of actual taxi destination signs
- Contribute by uploading photos of taxi signs they encounter
- See pricing information for routes
- Build a comprehensive database of taxi routes across South Africa

## Target Market
- Primary: Daily commuters in South African metropolitan areas (Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban, Pretoria)
- Secondary: Tourists and occasional travelers
- Tertiary: Taxi operators and transportation authorities

## Market Size
- 15+ million daily minibus taxi commuters in South Africa
- 200,000+ minibus taxis operating nationwide
- R40+ billion annual industry value

## Business Model
1. Freemium app with ad-supported free tier
2. Premium subscriptions for ad-free experience
3. Partnership opportunities with transportation companies
4. Data licensing to transportation authorities
5. In-app advertising (Google AdMob integrated)

## Competitive Advantage
- First-mover advantage in taxi sign crowdsourcing
- Community-driven content creation
- Real-time, user-verified information
- Localized for South African market
- Integration with Google Maps and Places API

## Key Metrics (Target)
- 100,000+ downloads in first year
- 50,000+ active monthly users
- 10,000+ taxi signs uploaded
- 4.0+ star rating on Google Play Store
- 60% user retention rate after 30 days

## Technology Stack

- Backend: Firebase (Authentication, Database, Storage)

- Analytics: Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics
